Coffee

Divine Nectar

This drink is only for those with refined tastes.

Be warned if you are not one of the chosen, it will not be a pleasant experience.

Ingredients

  • Adam's Ale
  • Instant Coffee (The best kind of coffee)
  • Disaccharides
  • Coffee Creamer (Wawa French Vanilla preferably)

Steps

  1. Pour as much instant coffee as preferred into your favorite coffee mug
  2. Dissolve instant coffee with small amount of cold/room temperature water. (Very important).
  3. Heat water in a pan until roaring boil.
  4. Pour boiling water into mug until about 80% filled with water.
  5. Dissolve as many disaccharides as desired as well as creamer.
  6. DONE! Enjoy :]
